Stock Making Principles ‚ñº

White Stock: Blanched bones, cold water start

Brown Stock: Roasted bones (400¬∞F oven)

Fish Stock: 45 mins max (bitter if longer)

Vegetable Stock: 1 hour simmer

Ratio: 1 lb bones : 1 qt water

Mirepoix ratio: 2:1:1 (onion:celery:carrot)

‚ö†Ô∏è Remember: Never boil! Always simmer. Skim regularly.
Plating Techniques ‚ñº

Clock Method: Protein at 6 o'clock, starch at 2, veg at 10

Height: Build up, not flat

Odd Numbers: 3 or 5 pieces looks better

White Space: Don't overcrowd the plate

Clean Rims: Wipe edges before service

Sauce: Under or beside, never on top

Garnish: Must be edible and relevant

Basic Chicken Stock ‚ñº

Ingredients:

  • 5 lbs chicken bones
  • 1 gallon cold water
  • 2 onions, roughly chopped
  • 2 carrots, roughly chopped
  • 2 celery stalks
  • Bay leaves, peppercorns, thyme

Instructions:

1. Blanch bones (3-5 min boil, rinse)

2. Cold water start, bring to simmer

3. Skim impurities regularly

4. Add mirepoix after 2 hours

5. Simmer total 4-6 hours

6. Strain through cheesecloth

üí° Tip: Never boil! Laging gentle simmer lang para clear ang stock.
Adobong Manok (Professional) ‚ñº

Ingredients (10 servings):

  • 3 kg chicken, portioned
  • 200ml soy sauce
  • 150ml vinegar
  • 20 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 10 bay leaves
  • 20g whole peppercorns
  • 100ml vegetable oil

Method:

1. Marinate chicken sa soy sauce, vinegar, garlic (2 hrs)

2. Sear chicken until golden

3. Add marinade, bay leaves, peppercorns

4. Simmer 30-40 minutes

5. Reduce sauce until thick

6. Season to taste

Kare-Kare (Professional Recipe) ‚ñº

Ingredients (10 servings):

  • 2 kg oxtail, cut into portions
  • 500g tripe, cleaned
  • 300g peanut butter
  • 100g ground toasted rice
  • 200g sitaw, cut 2"
  • 1 bundle pechay
  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • Bagoong alamang for serving

Method:

1. Boil oxtail and tripe until tender (2-3 hrs)

2. Make peanut sauce: mix peanut butter with stock

3. Add ground rice to thicken

4. Add meat, simmer 30 mins

5. Add vegetables, cook 5 mins

6. Serve with bagoong on the side

Pancit Canton (Commercial Kitchen) ‚ñº

Ingredients (15 servings):

  • 1 kg canton noodles
  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 300g shrimp, peeled
  • 400g mixed vegetables (carrots, cabbage, baguio beans)
  • 200ml soy sauce
  • 100ml oyster sauce
  • 10 cloves garlic, minced

Method:

1. Par-cook noodles, drain well

2. Stir-fry proteins separately

3. Stir-fry vegetables crisp-tender

4. Combine all, add noodles

5. Season with soy sauce and oyster sauce

6. Toss continuously, cook 3-5 mins
